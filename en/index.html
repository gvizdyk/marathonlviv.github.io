To participate in the competition you need:

Statement of good health

Statement of good health (medical certificate) confirming satisfactory state of your health is obligatory. Also we require a signed statement, which indicates that each participant takes part in competition at their own risk and responsibility.

Entry fee

You can pay the entry fee: by transferring money to the Privatbank card 4149 4978 3489 1931 (Pidluzhna Iryna Romanivna) before April 10, 2016, or in the competition's Secretariat on April 23, 2016. The entry fee is 200 UAH before February 15, 2016.

Age

Any person who is 18 years or older on the day of the event (April 24, 2016) can compete.

Regulations

Regulated time to overcome distance - 3 hours. Participants who will be unable to cover the distance in regulated time must take off their bib number and leave the competition route.

Health care

The competition organizers will provide medical services to the participants during the competition (doctor will be at the start and the finish). Route will be accompanied by an ambulance.

The route and running distance

Distance is 21,1 km.
The time will be measured using electronic system.

The route will be marked, as well as every kilometer of the distance. Volunteers and judges will help to organize movement on the road.

The competition program also includes 4 km charity run.

Registration

Each competitor must register for the competition by passing preliminary e-registration, or directly in the competition's Secretariat that will work on Aptil 23, 2016.

Admission of additional applications, registration of participants, and issuance of bib numbers will be held only on April 23, Saturday at 4 p.m. during the Pasta Party.

The final application for participation in the competition is: pre-registration, medical certificate, the signed statement, the entry fee payment.

Entry fee

You can pay the entry fee: transferring money to the Privatbank card 4149 4978 3489 1931 (Pidluzhna Iryna Romanivna) before April 10, 2016, or in the competition's Secretariat on April 23, 2016.
